Postgraduate tuition fees

The price you'll pay for postgraduate study at Southampton depends on the type of degree you're studying for and whether you're a home or overseas student.

Channel Island, Isle of Man and Irish students will be charged the same tuition fee as UK students.

If you're a Southampton alumni or in your last year of undergraduate study, you may be entitled to a 10% discount on your postgraduate fees.

Taught courses

You can find 2023 -entry tuition fee costs for full-time courses on our postgraduate taught course pages.

For other versions of our postgraduate taught courses, such as part-time, you can check the table below.

Defer your place

Students who defer their place will pay the tuition fees set for the academic year they start their studies.

Previous years' fees

If needed, you can access full fee information from previous years.

Funding your studies

You might be able to get help to cover some of your costs when you come to study at Southampton. This help can come from many different places and organisations.

Student finance

Find out about the government loans & financial support available to postgraduate students studying at Southampton.

Funding in your subject area

Our academic departments often offer funding in specific subject areas. This could be in the form of scholarships, bursaries or studentships.

Scholarships and awards

There are a range of scholarships and awards available to postgraduate students at Southampton. These are offered by governments, external organisations or the University.
