Exchange and study abroad

Boost your career opportunities by studying or working overseas as part of your university course. Explore new educational, social and cultural experiences.

University of Southampton students can apply to study for up to a year at one of our partner institutions.

Overseas students can come to Southampton on an exchange, or as part of the Study Abroad in Southampton scheme.
