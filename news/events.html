    Engagement & Advancement Events Team

    The Engagement & Advancement Events team supports events activity for both The Office of Development and Alumni Relations and the Communication and Marketing team. The team manages an annual programme of high profile events relating to brand development and engagement as well as supporting the Alumni strategy in the UK and internationally. The team also provides advice and guidance on event management for the University community.

    The team can be contacted via; events@southampton.ac.uk

    Or for events relating specifically to our Alumni Community via: ARevents@southampton.ac.uk

    More details on our alumni offering can be found here.

    Conference, Events & Hospitality

    The University’s Conference, Events and Hospitality Office is responsible for the management of conference/meeting rooms, accommodation, function rooms and associated catering and hospitality services across our University campuses, further information can be found on their website.

    The team can be contacted via;
    023 8059 2832
    hospitality@southampton.ac.uk

    Open Days & Campus tours

    Get a feel for the University by visiting our campuses at one of our Open Days, further information including how to register for a visit can be found here.

    The team can be contacted via;

    visit@southampton.ac.uk

    Union Southampton events

    The events team is part of the University of Southampton Students' Union and delivers the Unions' highest profile student events.

    The team can be contacted via;
